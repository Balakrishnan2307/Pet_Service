Our Story

PawCare was founded in 2016 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell and Michael Chen, two lifelong friends who shared a deep passion for animal welfare. What started as a small veterinary clinic has grown into a comprehensive pet care center serving thousands of families.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every pet deserves to be treated with the same love and care we give our own. This philosophy drives everything we do, from our state-of-the-art medical facilities to our cozy boarding suites.
